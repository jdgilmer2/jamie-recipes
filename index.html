Recipe #1 Buffalo Chicken Burritos 🍗

Makes: 10 burritos

Macros per burrito: 450 Calories • 60g Protein • 30g Carbs • 10g Fat

Ingredients 🌯

  • 60 oz chicken breast (1700g)
  • 1 cup diced onions (150g)
  • 1 tbsp garlic salt (15g)
  • 3 tbsp ranch seasoning (30g)
  • 0.5 tbsp black pepper (3g)
  • 0.5 cup buffalo sauce (120g)
  • 1 cup diced white onions (150g)
  • 3.5 cups fat free mozzarella (400g)
  • Fresh chives (10g)
  • 2.5 cups cooked white rice (500g)
  • 10 large low calorie tortillas

High Protein Buffalo Sauce 🌶️

  • 2.5 cups cottage cheese (0% fat) (560g)
  • 4 oz reduced fat cream cheese (115g)
  • 0.5 tbsp garlic salt (7g)
  • 0.5 tbsp black pepper (3g)
  • 0.5 tbsp onion powder (3g)
  • 0.5 cup buffalo sauce (120g)
  • 2 tbsp honey (40g)
  • 1 cup fat free milk (240g)

Freezing Instructions ❄️

  1. Wrap burritos in aluminum foil, or place in an airtight freezer bag.
  2. Freeze up to 3 months.

Reheating Instructions 🔥

  1. Take out a frozen burrito and wrap it in a wet paper towel.
  2. Microwave on a plate for 5–6 minutes.
  3. Optional: toast in a pan or air fry for extra crispiness.
Recipe #2 Chipotle Honey Chicken Burritos 🌯

Makes: 10 burritos

Into the Crock Pot

  • 60 oz chicken breast (1700g)
  • 2 tbsp garlic salt (30g)
  • 1 tbsp onion powder (7g)
  • 1 can chipotle peppers, 7 oz (200g)
  • 3 tbsp honey (60g)

Sauce

  • 1 cup cottage cheese (0% fat) (225g)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce (240g)
  • 4 garlic cloves (12g)
  • 0.5 cup fat free milk (120g)

After the Sauce Cooks

  • 1 cup dry rice (180g), cooked

Add Last When Building Burritos

  • 5 cups fat free mozzarella
  • 10 low-calorie tortillas (example: Extreme Wellness wraps, 110 cal each)

Serving Tip

Dip with your favorite hot sauce.
